Carlos_Fabián

A recent Miami transplant, Carlos Fabián is a multidisciplinary artist from Caracas, Venezuela that works in the performing and visual arts as a director, actor, videographer, and creative producer, based in Miami since 2019. With a main focus on theater and film, his artistic experience has allowed him to take on many roles in different projects, including assistant director, art director, stage manager, set and costume designer, performer and teacher.

He studied philosophy at the Universidad Central de Venezuela (Central University of Venezuela) and at the same time he trained as an actor in the prestigious experimental theater workshop at “Centro TET” (Centro de Creación Artística TET) from 2010-2015. He also studied and worked closely with directors Orlando Arocha and Diana Volpe at the renowned theater company “La Caja de Fósforos” (The Matchbox Theatre) based in Caracas, space where he developed strong skills in stage direction and production.

As a very recent videographer, Fabián’s work is based on independent collaborations with stylists, make-up artists, designers, musicians and performers by creating short and long format video pieces as director of photography and editor. Since 2020, he has been developing the project PLICA, a Miami-based art project in collaboration with artist Mikhael Scarr that addresses topics as politics in the post-truth era, the artist's role and identity in society. The project focuses on creating original drawings, books, designs and installations.
