Current Projects

Theater Projects

Fabián has worked as a director, assistant director, stage manager, teacher, designer, producer and actor in many different theater productions and plays written by national and international playwrights in his hometown Caracas. In Miami, he worked with companies as Miami New Drama, Tectonic Theater Project, Main Street Players and Open Doors Productions.


He has directed plays as “Tom At The Farm” by Michel Marc Bouchard (Awarded as Best Director in the III Festival of Young Directors of Trasnocho Cultural - Caracas), “Gruesome Playground Injuries” by Rajiv Joseph, “Woyzeck” by Georg Büchner, and the short story “The Fire, The Charcoal Girl And The Monster Of The Tallahassee River” by Elvis Chaveinte. In 2020, he co-directed alongside actress Mónica Quintero the work-in-progress “De: Circe” for the first online international festival of monologues by Waterpeople Theater based in Chicago.


His project LITTLE SPACES, an outdoors theatrical project that uses real testimonies to explore relevant topics about the city and its individuals, is one of Knight Foundation’s KNIGHT NEW WORK 2020 winning project ideas.
